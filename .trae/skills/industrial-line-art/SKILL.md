# Industrial Line Art Processor

## Overview

This skill processes industrial line art and generates relief patterns based on aesthetic references. It follows a structured workflow to ensure precise preservation of structural elements while applying aesthetic styles.

## Input Assets

- **structural_reference** (Figure 1 Industrial Line Art): Preserves outer frame boundary and central rectangular area structure
- **aesthetic_reference** (Figure 2 Inspiration): Extracts style, patterns, textures, and chromatic elements for relief design
- **material_reference** (Aged Brass Material Specs): Preserves aged brass characteristics with matte finish and polished relief details

## Workflow

1. **Step 1: Structural Analysis**
   - Parse structural elements from Figure 1 with strict precision
   - Identify outer frame boundary and central rectangular area dimensions

2. **Step 2: Aesthetic Extraction**
   - Extract style, chromatic narrative, patterns, and textures from Figure 2
   - Prepare these elements for application within the central rectangular area of Figure 1

3. **Step 3: Composite Generation**
   - Apply aesthetic elements from Figure 2 onto the central rectangular area of Figure 1
   - Ensure natural fit with seamless integration and no visible artifacts
   - Configure four distinct design variations in a 2x2 grid
   - Preserve complete structural integrity from Figure 1
   - Apply material properties from material_reference
   - Generate coherent light reflections and surface textures

## Output

- 4 high-quality industrial relief art images in a 2x2 grid
- Each image reveals the relief's overall design and fine details
- Consistent structural framework and material characteristics across all variations

## Usage

Invoke this skill when you need to:
- Create industrial product patterns from line art
- Generate relief art designs
- Apply aesthetic styles to technical drawings
- Produce multiple design variations from a single line art source
